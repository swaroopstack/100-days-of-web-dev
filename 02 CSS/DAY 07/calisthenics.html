Calisthenics Workout Split

Day-wise Bodyweight Calisthenics Workout
Day Exercise Sets Reps / Time
Day 1 - Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps) Push-ups 3-4 12-20
Diamond Push-ups 3 8-12
Pike Push-ups 3 10-12
Dips (using chairs) 3 10-15
Optional: Wall Handstand Hold 2 20-30 sec
Day 2 - Pull (Back, Biceps) Pull-ups 3-4 5-12
Chin-ups 3 6-10
Australian Pull-ups 3 10-15
Inverted Rows under a table or bar 3 10-12
Day 3 - Legs & Core Squats 3-4 15-20
Lunges 3 12 per leg
Jump Squats 3 15-20
Calf Raises 3 10-15
Plank 3 30-60 sec
Side Plank 3 30-45 sec per side
Day 4 - Full Body / Skill Burpees 3 10-15
Mountain Climbers 3 20-30
Handstand Practice 2-3 20-40 sec

Optional Days 5-6

  • Repeat weakest muscle groups or focus on mobility, stretching, and cardio.
  • Can do light jogging, jump rope, or yoga.

Tips for Progression

  1. Increase reps or sets gradually each week.
  2. Slow down movements to increase time under tension.
  3. Focus on form over quantity to prevent injuries.
